About enough HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to put this website together.

Passionate about crafting stories that are visually and narratively engaging. My experience spans online journalistic pieces, feature length-documentary, and promotional videos, covering such topics as the US criminal justice system, the undocumented community, and skateboarding. My work has been shown at the Freedom Film Festival and United Nations Headquarters, and has been recognised by the Midwest Chapter of the National Academy of Television Arts & Sciences.

I've been fortunate to learn from people of diverse backgrounds, and I look forward to learning more.

Videography reel of documentary and narrative works.

Cinematography, editing, and motion graphics.

Out of the Shadows is a short documentary about Quinto Imperio, a Cumbia band from Chicago’s Back of the Yards neighbourhood. It is a story of immigrants who are undocumented, unapologetic, and unafraid to fight for their future and for the future of youth from a community that is often misunderstood.
